Introduction

The ReelSmartMotionBlur technology is now available in Autodesk Softimage|XSI

RSMB for xsi is composed of two ufo's that works in the FXtree :

  • RSMB : Automatically add more natural-looking motion blur to a sequence using ReelSmart Motion Blur. Our tracking technology is at the heart of ReelSmart Motion Blur, so there is no handwork involved. Of course you can add as little or as much blurring as you need, and even remove some motion blur. You can even can blur one sequence by using the motion from another for very interesting effects
  • RSMBVectors : Blurs images using motion vectors images. In some cases can be used as an alternative to 3D motion blur.

How ReelSmart Motion Blur Works?

The plug-in calculates the motion between two successive frames in a sequence, calculates the motion from one frame to the next, and applies blur based on the calculated motion. No motion between two frames, no blur. This is useful to know when trying to figure out why RSMB doesn't apply blur to frames from a 3:2 pulldown sequence or animation done on "2"s, etc. The plugin expects deinterlaced material.

ReelSmart Motion Blur supports both 8, 16 bits and floating point pixel processing. The latest information about RE:Vision Effects products is always available on our website.

Please Note, when the plugin is loaded in FXtree, a menu item "About RSMB" shows up in XSI main Help menu. This will give you easy access to the latest info, doc and tutorials.
